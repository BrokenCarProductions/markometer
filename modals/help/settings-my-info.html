Update your personal information in the system, change your password, update your time zone, and set your default account (if you're in multiple accounts). Details:

  • First Name - Your first name (required)
  • Last Name - Your last name (required)
  • Title - Your job title
  • Company - The company or business you work for (required)
  • Office # - Your office telephone number at your company
  • Cell # - Your personal or company cell phone number
  • Email - Your email address for use in the system (this will also be your account login/username) (required)
  • Default Account - If you're in multiple accounts, you can select which account you see by default when you login to the system. You can switch between accounts with the Account dropdown at the top right of the screen.
  • Time Zone - The time zone you live in. We'll use this information for recording notes and events in the system that have a time stamp.
  • Old Password - Type the existing password you use to access the system. Only type this if you want to change your password.
  • New Password - Type the new password you want use to access the system. It must be at least 6 characters long.
  • Confirm New Password - Retype the new password you want to use to access the system. It must match the new password you typed above.
